How To Connect

Connecting to the IRC network couldn't be easier. While some may prefer the ease of the webchat interface, others will prefer the traditional IRC client. For those of you wishing to use a client, you can use your primary roundrobin, irc.voxinfinitus.net on port 6667. We recommend using SSL/TLS which is acessible on port 6697 or 9999. Make sure your client is configured to use SSL/TLS. You may need to set this on a server-by-server basis.

Tor Hidden Service

We are particularly strong advocates of privacy and anonymity, for this reason all connecting clients display an encrypted hostmask, rather than their actual IP. However if you prefer stronger anonymity, we offer a Tor Hidden Service at o4ukkv3l2be242za.onion on port 6667. Configuration of Tor and your client is beyond the scope of this article. Please consult the documentation associated with your client.

Feedback

Have some questions or comments? Great! We'd love to hear from you. The best way to get in touch with us is on the IRC network. Generally at least one staff member is active at any given time. Connecting to the network couldn't be easier, we have a webchat client available. If you prefer to use your favorite client, you can connect to irc.voxinfinitus.net. We use the standard port, 6667, for regular connections, and port 6697 for SSL/TLS connections.

Twitter

We post regular updates to Twitter regarding the status of the network. For up-to-the-minute updates and announcements follow @VoxInfinitus. See you there!
