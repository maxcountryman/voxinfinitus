Vox Infinitus

A Community

Vox Infinitus is a community for people seeking to preserve freedom of expression, specifically in the context of modern information technology.

The New Frontier?

The Web is a bit of a new frontier, so to speak. However it has already proved to be one of the most influential. In large part we believe this is because of the unfettered access new ideas have had to resources.

We think the Web is awesome! Seemingly its potential is enormous. So we want to advocate for things that we believe will maintain the qualities that have allowed the Internet to be what it is today.

Internet Relay Chat

IRC is the primary context in which our community happens. For detailed connection instructions check out the IRC section on the About page or use the Connect button directly below to connect with the Web interface.

All hostmasks are always encrypted, this means peers can't see your IP. However if you prefer stronger anonymity, we do offer a Tor hidden service.
